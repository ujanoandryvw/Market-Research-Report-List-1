<p><p>The Trona market encompasses three main types of mining methods: Conventional Continuous Mining, Room Mining, and Pillar Mining. Conventional Continuous Mining involves the use of continuous miner machines to extract trona in a streamlined process, maximizing efficiency. Room Mining creates large open spaces, or “rooms,” allowing for increased extraction rates while leaving pillars for support. Pillar Mining focuses on extracting resources while preserving structural integrity by leaving behind substantial pillars of trona to maintain mine stability, optimizing resource recovery.</p></p>
