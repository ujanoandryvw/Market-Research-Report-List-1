<p><p>The prepaid communication market consists of two primary types: monopolized and accessorial service markets. In contrast, the accessorial service market involves supplementary services offered alongside core prepaid products, creating pathways for competition and innovation. This market allows multiple providers to enhance customer experience with add-ons like data plans or international calling, fostering a diverse ecosystem of services.</p></p>
