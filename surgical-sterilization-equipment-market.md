<p><strong>The Surgical Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Temperature</li><li>Low Temperature</li><li>Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical sterilization equipment market is categorized into three main types: high temperature, low temperature, and radiation sterilization. High-temperature sterilization typically uses steam or dry heat to eliminate pathogens, making it effective for metal instruments. Low-temperature sterilization employs chemicals like ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide, ideal for heat-sensitive items. Radiation sterilization, utilizing gamma rays or electron beams, is suitable for single-use medical products. Each method is chosen based on the material compatibility, required sterility assurance, and specific application in healthcare settings.</p></p>
