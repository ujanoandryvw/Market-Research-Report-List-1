<p><p>The ventilated stretch film market comprises two primary types: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is designed for hand application, featuring punctures that enhance breathability for products like fresh produce, allowing air circulation while maintaining protection. In contrast, perforated machine film is utilized in automated packaging processes, offering greater efficiency and consistent performance. Both types cater to specific packaging needs, ensuring optimal preservation and extending the shelf life of various goods while preventing moisture build-up.</p></p>
