<p><p>The tanning booth market is categorized into three main types based on lamp pressure: low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure lamps. Low-pressure lamps emit UVB rays, primarily targeting melanin production and requiring longer exposure times for tanning. Medium-pressure lamps offer a balanced mix of UVA and UVB rays for quicker results, making them popular among users. High-pressure lamps primarily emit UVA rays, providing deeper tanning effects in shorter sessions while ensuring less risk of burning. Each type caters to specific tanning needs and preferences.</p></p>
