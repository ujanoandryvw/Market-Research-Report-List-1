<p><p>The cell proliferation kits market includes several types designed to assess cell viability. Fluorometric cell viability kits utilize fluorescence signals to measure live cells, providing high sensitivity. Colorimetric cell viability kits use color changes to indicate cell activity, offering an easy and cost-effective method for analysis. Bioluminescent cell viability kits measure light produced by living cells, allowing for highly sensitive detection. Each kit type serves distinct applications in research and clinical settings, enabling precise evaluation of cellular health and growth.</p></p>
