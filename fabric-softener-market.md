<p><p>The fabric softener market encompasses primarily two types: liquid fabric softener and fabric softener sheets. Liquid fabric softeners are concentrated solutions added during the rinse cycle of laundry, enhancing softness, reducing static cling, and imparting pleasant fragrances. They penetrate fibers for deep conditioning. Fabric softener sheets, on the other hand, are used in the dryer to achieve similar benefits by reducing static and adding scent to clothes. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and laundry processes, enhancing overall fabric care.</p></p>
