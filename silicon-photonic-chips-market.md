<p><p>The silicon photonic chips market is primarily segmented by the type of materials used, namely silicon nitride and silicon oxide. Silicon nitride offers high thermal stability and low loss in optical signals, making it ideal for advanced communication systems. In contrast, silicon oxide is known for its compatibility with silicon-based technologies, enabling cost-effective integration with existing semiconductor processes. Both materials are essential for developing high-performance photonic devices, catering to applications in telecommunications, data centers, and sensor technologies.</p></p>
