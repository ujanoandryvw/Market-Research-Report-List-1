<p><p>Direct Air Capture (DAC) technology captures carbon dioxide directly from the atmosphere using specialized methods. The market is primarily divided into two types: physical absorption in liquid media, where CO2 is dissolved in a liquid solvent, allowing for efficient separation and subsequent storage; and adsorption on solid media, which relies on materials that attract and bind CO2 molecules. Both methods aim to reduce atmospheric CO2 levels, contributing to climate change mitigation efforts while offering diverse applications and scalability options.</p></p>
