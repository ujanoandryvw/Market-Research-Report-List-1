<p><p>The commercial digital door lock market features various types, including magnetic stripe locks, electromechanical door locks, and electric strike locks. Magnetic stripe locks utilize swipe cards for access, making them convenient and easy to manage. Electromechanical door locks combine electronic components with mechanical mechanisms, offering enhanced security and flexibility in access control. Electric strike locks work with a standard lock and are activated electronically, allowing for remote unlocking and integration with security systems, thereby enhancing safety and efficiency in commercial settings.</p></p>
