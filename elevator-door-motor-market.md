<p><strong>The Elevator Door-motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inverter Door-motor</li><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Elevator Door-motor market comprises two primary types: Inverter Door-motors and Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motors. Inverter Door-motors use variable frequency drives to control motor speed and torque, offering efficient and smooth door operations, ideal for high-traffic environments. Conversely, Permanent Magnet Synchronous Door-motors utilize permanent magnets for efficient and precise control, resulting in energy savings and reduced maintenance. Both technologies enhance performance and reliability, catering to the evolving demands of modern elevator systems.</p></p>
