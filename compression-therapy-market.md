<p><ul><li>Static Compression Therapy</li><li>Dynamic Compression Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compression Therapy Market is divided into two main types: Static Compression Therapy and Dynamic Compression Therapy. Static Compression Therapy involves the application of constant pressure to limbs using devices like bandages or stockings, aiding in reducing edema and improving circulation. In contrast, Dynamic Compression Therapy employs intermittent pressure through devices that inflate and deflate, promoting venous return while providing enhanced mobility. Both methods are vital in treating conditions such as deep vein thrombosis and lymphedema, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
