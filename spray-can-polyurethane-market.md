<p><p>The spray can polyurethane market is primarily divided into two types: oil-based polyurethane and water-based polyurethane. Oil-based polyurethane offers a durable and glossy finish, making it suitable for high-traffic areas, but has longer drying times and stronger odors. In contrast, water-based polyurethane dries quickly, has lower odor levels, and is more environmentally friendly, making it ideal for indoor projects. Both types provide protective coatings for wood surfaces, though their application and environmental impacts differ significantly.</p></p>
