<p><p>The radar market for autonomous vehicles is categorized into Short-Range Radar (SRR), Medium-Range Radar (MRR), and Long-Range Radar (LRR). SRR is used for close-range applications like parking assistance and collision avoidance. MRR operates in the mid-range for detecting obstacles during lane changing and adaptive cruise control. LRR covers longer distances for functions such as highway driving and environmental scanning, enhancing situational awareness by providing accurate data about surroundings at various ranges.</p></p>
