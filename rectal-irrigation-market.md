<p><p>The rectal irrigation market comprises three main types: cone systems, balloon systems, and bed systems. Cone systems utilize a conical shape to facilitate fluid delivery, allowing for targeted irrigation. Balloon systems involve a balloon mechanism that expands within the rectum to hold fluid securely, improving retention and effectiveness during the procedure. Bed systems are designed for use with patients in a lying position, providing comfort and stability while ensuring efficient irrigation. Each system caters to different patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
