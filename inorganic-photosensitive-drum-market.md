<p><p>The inorganic photosensitive drum market comprises various types, including selenium, selenium alloy, cadmium sulfide, and amorphous silicon. Selenium drums are known for their excellent photoconductive properties and are widely used in laser printers. Selenium alloy drums enhance performance and durability. Cadmium sulfide, though less common, provides good sensitivity and stability. Amorphous silicon offers high-resolution capabilities and is often used in digital applications. Each type has distinct advantages, catering to different printing technologies and requirements.</p></p>
