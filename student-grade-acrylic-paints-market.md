<p><ul><li>Fluid Acrylics</li><li>Heavy-Body Acrylics</li><li>Slow-Drying Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The student grade acrylic paints market consists of three main types: Fluid Acrylics, Heavy-Body Acrylics, and Slow-Drying Agents. Fluid Acrylics are highly pigmented and have a thin consistency, making them ideal for pouring and detailed work. Heavy-Body Acrylics offer a thick, buttery texture, perfect for impasto techniques and retaining brush strokes. Slow-Drying Agents are additives that extend the drying time of acrylic paints, allowing for longer manipulation and blending, enhancing the flexibility and creativity for students and beginner artists.</p></p>
