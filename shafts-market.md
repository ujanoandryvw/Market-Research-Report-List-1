<p><p>Shafts play a crucial role in various mechanical systems, with different types serving specific functions in motion transfer. Splined shafts connect components by interlocking grooves, ensuring precision in alignment and power transmission. Precision shafts maintain tight tolerances for high-performance applications. Universal joints allow for flexible movement between shafts at varying angles. Hollow shafts reduce weight while maintaining strength, making them ideal for rotational applications. Other shaft types may include tapered, keyway, or custom designs to meet specific engineering needs.</p></p>
