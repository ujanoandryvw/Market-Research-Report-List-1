<p><p>The fullerene nanotubes market comprises three primary types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT), and Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT). SWNTs consist of a single graphene cylinder, offering exceptional electrical and mechanical properties, making them ideal for various applications. DWNTs feature two concentric nanotubes, enhancing performance while providing improved stability. MWNTs, comprising multiple layers, possess high conductivity and structural strength, suitable for composites and electronics. Each type serves distinct applications in fields such as materials science, electronics, and biomedical engineering.</p></p>
