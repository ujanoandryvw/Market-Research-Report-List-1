<p><p>The Medical Instrument Air System market comprises several types based on redundancy and capacity: Duplex systems feature dual compressors to ensure reliability, providing a back-up in case one fails. Triplex systems incorporate three compressors, enhancing redundancy and ensuring consistent airflow for critical applications. Quadplex systems utilize four compressors, offering the highest level of reliability and performance, suitable for large facilities with significant demand. Each type is designed to maintain sterile air supply for medical instruments while ensuring operational continuity.</p></p>
