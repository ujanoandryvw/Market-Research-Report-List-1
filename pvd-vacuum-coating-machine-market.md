<p><p>The PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) Vacuum Coating Machine market is primarily divided into three types: evaporation equipment, sputtering equipment, and others. Evaporation equipment utilizes thermal energy to vaporize materials that then condense on substrates, forming thin films. Sputtering equipment, on the other hand, employs ion bombardment to eject material from a target onto the substrate, yielding high-quality coatings. Other equipment may include techniques like ion plating and laser ablation, catering to specific applications and industries, enhancing versatility in coating solutions.</p></p>
