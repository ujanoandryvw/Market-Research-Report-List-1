<p><p>The Virtual Commissioning market encompasses two primary types: Plant and Process Simulation, and Robotics and Automation Simulation. Plant and Process Simulation focuses on modeling and optimizing entire production systems, enabling businesses to test and validate processes before physical implementation, enhancing efficiency and reducing downtime. Conversely, Robotics and Automation Simulation specifically targets robotic systems and automated processes, allowing for precise programming and performance testing in a virtual environment. Both types improve project outcomes, reduce costs, and accelerate time-to-market through predictive analysis and scenario testing.</p></p>
