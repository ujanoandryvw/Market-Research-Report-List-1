<p><p>The low emission vehicles market encompasses various types aimed at reducing environmental impact. Other market segments include Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), which can operate on both electric and gasoline power, and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs) that convert hydrogen into electricity, emitting only water vapor.</p></p>
